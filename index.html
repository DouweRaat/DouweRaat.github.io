LEGO-VR

I worked on this project in the first half of 2022. In this VR-game the player is able to create their own world by placing giant LEGO bricks. It was a challenge to find a good system for the placement, but I eventually settled on a laser pointing towards the desired spot, together with a preview of the brick. The player is also able to change the color and shape of the bricks with the controllers. The game was made with Unity and the SteamVR plugin.

Hologram games

This is a project I made together with Lawrence Zhu for our high school thesis. We made 2 games to answer the question: "To what extend are holograms suitable for videogames?" The games we made are named Pong-3D and Tanks-3D. These are both 3D versions of previously existing games: Pong and a flash-game named Tanks. We made each game with 3 different display options: a regular laptop screen, projected in a plastic pyramid and projected in the HOLOFIL-cardboard.

Quantum Quest

Quantum Quest is a web class for high school students, which I took part in in november/december 2020. The course is organized by the University of Amsterdam, QuSoft, Ruhr University Bochum and CASA and teaches the mathematics behind Quantum Computers. The group I worked with and I got some of the highest grades in the course, which we each recieved two books for.
