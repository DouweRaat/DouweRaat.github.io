LEGO-VR

LEGO-VR is the project I worked on in the first half of 2022. In this VR-game the player is able to place LEGO bricks and walk around in the environment they created. It was a challenge to find a good system for the placement, but I eventually settled on a laser pointing towards the desired spot, together with a preview of the brick. The player is also able to change the color and shape of the bricks. The game was made with Unity and the SteamVR plugin. The game is available here.

Hologram games

This is a project I made together with Lawrence Zhu for our high school thesis. We made 2 games to answer the question: "To what extend are holograms suitable for videogames?" The games we made are named Pong-3D and Tanks-3D. These are both 3D versions of previously existing games: Pong and a flash-game named Tanks. We made each game with 3 different display options: a regular laptop screen, projected in a plastic pyramid and projected in the HOLOFIL-cardboard. The games are available here (Pong-3D) and here (Tanks-3D) and the paper (Dutch) is available here.
